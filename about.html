Animal Classification From Images

Created By:
Yuvraj Kaushik, A Student Exploring AI And Web Development.

How Does It Work:

You Can Upload, Or Drag And Drop, An Image Of Any Animal.
The Model Is A Pre-Trained Convolutional Neural Network (CNN),
Which Will Analyze The Image You Uploaded/Dropped.
The Prediction Animal Name With A Confidence Score Will Be Displayed Instantly.
